Digital Marketing Lead Management

Paid Advertising Integration & Sales Automation

Comprehensive management of paid digital advertising leads from Meta, Google, and LinkedIn, featuring automated lead ingestion, opportunity creation, and intelligent sales assignment through seamless Salesforce integration.

Digital Marketing Partnership

Collaborated closely with the marketing department to establish a sophisticated lead management system that captures, processes, and converts paid digital advertising prospects into revenue-generating opportunities. The system automatically ingests leads from major advertising platforms and seamlessly integrates them into our sales pipeline with intelligent routing and immediate assignment protocols.

Collaborative Workflows

  • Campaign Planning - Joint strategy sessions for lead capture optimization and sales alignment
  • Performance Reviews - Weekly conversion analysis and campaign performance evaluations
  • Audience Development - Leveraging sales insights for improved targeting and messaging
  • Budget Optimization - Data-driven recommendations for advertising spend allocation
  • Creative Testing - A/B testing coordination for ad creative and landing page optimization

Shared Metrics & Reporting

  • Real-time Dashboards - Live conversion tracking and campaign performance monitoring
  • Attribution Reporting - Complete customer journey visibility from ad impression to sale
  • ROI Analysis - Platform-specific and campaign-level return on investment calculations
  • Lead Quality Metrics - Conversion rates, sales cycle length, and average deal size tracking
  • Optimization Recommendations - Data-driven suggestions for campaign improvements

System Capabilities

  • Real-time lead ingestion from Meta Ads, Google Ads, and LinkedIn Campaign Manager
  • Automated opportunity creation with campaign source attribution and UTM tracking
  • Intelligent lead scoring and routing based on platform source and engagement quality
  • Instant sales team notification and assignment with predefined territory rules
  • Comprehensive ROI tracking and campaign performance analysis across all platforms
  • Marketing-sales alignment through shared dashboards and conversion reporting
