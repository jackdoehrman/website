Program Overview & Philosophy

Led the comprehensive internship program for the Business Intelligence & Strategy team, serving as primary mentor and supervisor for developing young professionals entering the sports analytics industry. The program emphasizes both technical skill development and professional growth through structured projects, mentoring relationships, and immersive workplace experiences.

Mentorship Philosophy

  • Foster both technical competency and professional development through hands-on project experience
  • Provide comprehensive exposure to real-world business intelligence and strategic analysis workflows
  • Create meaningful learning opportunities through shadowing, collaboration, and independent project ownership
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills through guided analysis and strategic decision-making
  • Build confidence and professional presence through presentation opportunities and stakeholder interaction
  • Prepare interns for successful full-time career transitions with industry-relevant skills and experience

Program Structure & Timeline

Designed a comprehensive 12-week program with progressive skill development, increasing responsibility, and diverse project exposure to maximize learning outcomes and professional growth opportunities.

12-Week Development Timeline

Week 1-2
Orientation & Foundation
Company overview, team introductions, tool training (Salesforce, Tableau, SQL), and initial project assignments with comprehensive onboarding.
Week 3-4
Skill Development
Hands-on training in data analysis, visualization creation, and business intelligence fundamentals with guided practice exercises.
Week 5-6
Project Implementation
Begin independent project work with regular check-ins, shadowing senior analyst activities, and participating in team meetings.
Week 7-8
Advanced Analysis
Complex data analysis projects, stakeholder presentation preparation, and increased responsibility for deliverable quality and timeline management.
Week 9-10
Strategic Projects
Lead strategic analysis initiatives, present findings to department leadership, and contribute to business decision-making processes.
Week 11-12
Capstone & Transition
Complete capstone project presentation, performance evaluation, career planning discussions, and full-time opportunity assessment.
