Season Ticket Member - "At-Risk" Scoring

Predictive Modeling Project by Jack Doehrman

This project was designed to identify season ticket members (STMs) at risk of non-renewal using historical behaviors, CRM activity, and engagement data.

Overview

The STM Scoring model was developed to support retention efforts by scoring each STM’s likelihood of renewal on a 0–100 scale.

Data Sources

  • CRM activity logs (Salesforce)
  • Ticket scans and attendance
  • Historical renewal behavior
  • Marketing and email engagement (SFMC)

Methodology

The model was built using logistic regression and calibrated with historical seasons. Key features included tenure, last touchpoint, and engagement velocity.

Results & Impact

Accounts flagged "at risk" showed 4x higher churn rate. Reps used the score to prioritize outreach, increasing renewal rate in low-score segments by 12%.
