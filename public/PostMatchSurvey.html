Post-Match Survey Analytics

Fan Experience Data Collection & Analysis

A comprehensive survey system deployed to capture feedback from attendees at Houston Dynamo FC, Houston Dash, and Shell Energy Stadium events, providing actionable insights to enhance the fan experience.

Project Overview

The Post-Match Survey project was developed to systematically collect and analyze feedback from event attendees across all Shell Energy Stadium events. This initiative provides real-time insights into fan satisfaction, operational efficiency, and areas for improvement.

Key Objectives

  • Measure overall fan satisfaction and Net Promoter Score (NPS)
  • Identify operational pain points and service gaps
  • Track performance trends across different event types
  • Enable data-driven decision making for fan experience improvements

Implementation & Data Collection

The survey is automatically triggered and sent to attendees within 24-48 hours after each event, ensuring high response rates while the experience is still fresh in attendees' minds.

Survey Components

  • Overall Experience Rating - 5-point scale satisfaction measurement
  • Net Promoter Score - Likelihood to recommend to friends/family
  • Operational Areas - Parking, concessions, security, cleanliness
  • Event-Specific Questions - Tailored based on event type (Dynamo, Dash, concerts)
  • Open-Ended Feedback - Qualitative insights and suggestions
  • Demographics - Attendance frequency, seating section, group size

Data Analysis & Insights

Survey responses are processed in real-time, with automated dashboards providing immediate visibility into fan sentiment and operational performance. Advanced analytics identify trends, correlations, and predictive indicators.

Key Findings & Impact

  • Concession Wait Times - Identified as primary pain point, leading to staffing optimization
  • Parking Experience - Data-driven improvements to traffic flow and signage
  • Event Type Variations - Distinct satisfaction patterns between soccer matches and concerts
  • Season Trends - Correlation between team performance and overall satisfaction scores
  • Loyalty Indicators - NPS scores predict season ticket renewal likelihood

Technical Implementation

The survey system integrates with multiple data sources and platforms to ensure comprehensive data collection and seamless analysis workflows.

Technology Stack

  • Survey Platform - Qualtrics for survey design and distribution
  • Data Integration - Automated APIs connecting to CRM and ticketing systems
  • Analytics Platform - PowerBI for real-time dashboarding and reporting
  • Data Warehouse - Centralized storage for historical trend analysis
  • Automation - Triggered email campaigns based on attendance data

Data Quality & Validation

  • Automated data validation rules to ensure response quality
  • Duplicate response detection and handling
  • Real-time monitoring of response rates and completion metrics
  • Regular data audits and cleansing procedures

Business Impact & Results

The Post-Match Survey project has become a cornerstone of the organization's fan experience strategy, directly influencing operational decisions and investment priorities.

Measurable Outcomes

  • 15% increase in overall fan satisfaction scores year-over-year
  • 25% reduction in concession wait times through targeted staffing
  • $200K+ cost savings from data-driven operational optimizations
  • Enhanced decision-making for facility improvements and vendor management
  • Improved NPS scores correlating with increased season ticket renewals
